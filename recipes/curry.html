Curry

Description

Discover the secrets of mastering the perfect balance of heat, fragrance, and richness as you navigate the diverse array of curries from around the world. Our recipe, meticulously curated and elegantly presented, promises to elevate your home cooking to an art form.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons of curry paste (choose your preferred level of spiciness)
  • 1 pound (450 grams) of your choice of protein (chicken, beef, tofu, shrimp, etc.), cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of assorted vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, peas, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Cooked rice or naan bread for serving>

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients

    Begin by finely chopping the onion, mincing the garlic, and grating the fresh ginger. Cut your choice of protein (chicken, beef, tofu, shrimp, etc.) into bite-sized pieces. If you're using fresh vegetables, wash and chop them into your desired shapes and sizes.

  2. Heat the Vegetable Oil

    In a large skillet or saucepan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat until it shimmers.

  3. Sauté the Aromatics

    Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger to the hot oil. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the onions turn translucent and aromatic.

  4. Add the Curry Paste

    Stir in the curry paste of your choice, adjusting the amount based on your preferred level of spiciness.

  5. Sear the Protein

    Now, introduce the bite-sized pieces of protein to the skillet and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides, which usually takes about 5 minutes.

  6. Incorporate the Spices

    Sprinkle in the curry powder, ground turmeric, ground cumin, ground coriander, and paprika. Stir well to coat the protein and aromatics with these aromatic spices.

  7. Introduce Tomatoes

    Pour in the can of diced tomatoes, including the liquid. Stir and allow the mixture to simmer for 5-7 minutes until it thickens slightly.

  8. Pour in Coconut Milk and Broth

    Add the can of coconut milk and the cup of chicken or vegetable broth. Stir to combine all the ingredients and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.

  9. Simmer and Add Vegetables

    Let the curry simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the protein is cooked through and the flavors meld together. During the last 5-7 minutes of cooking, add the assorted vegetables, cooking until they are tender yet still vibrant.

  10. Season and Serve

    Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed. Remove the curry from heat and serve it hot, garnished with fresh cilantro leaves if desired.

  11. Accompaniments

    To complete your meal, serve your delicious curry with cooked rice or warm naan bread. Enjoy your homemade curry, a flavorful fusion of spices and ingredients that will delight your taste buds and transport you to a world of culinary delight!
